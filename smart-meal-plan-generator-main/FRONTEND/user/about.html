Our Story

Smart Lishe was created because most nutrition advice doesn't work for Kenyans. It's built around foods we don't eat, ingredients we can't afford, and habits that don't fit our culture.

We're changing that. Every meal plan, recipe, and recommendation in Smart Lishe is built around real Kenyan food — ugali, sukuma wiki, githeri, nyama choma, omena, and everything else you grew up eating.

Our AI understands the nutritional value of local ingredients, suggests swaps that work for your budget, and gives you guidance that actually fits your lifestyle.

Whether you're managing diabetes, trying to lose weight, or simply want to eat better — we're here to help. No imported supplements. No unrealistic meal plans. Just real food, real science, and real results.
